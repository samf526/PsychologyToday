            Greetings! I am primarily a group and individual psychotherapist specializing in childhood dysfunctional family trauma. If you are seeking a general light talk therapist, I may not be right for you. I work from a very specific and effective model regarding my client&#039;s childhood that promotes deep and lastly change and healing. It calls for emotionally motivated work. My clients experience deep shifts in negative core beliefs, are able to reclaim intimacy, profoundly change their relationship with themselves, gain a sense of movement and recovery, and reclaim a lost sense of joy.
        </div>
    
                    <div class="statementPara">
            The model that I use is based upon inner child work where my clients learning what is functional v. dysfunctional and begin the process of &quot;re parenting&quot; themselves. Learning about this concept is an amazing journey and John Bradshaw&#039;s &quot;Home Coming&quot; is an excellent resource.
        </div>
    
                    <div class="statementPara">
            This work is very unique and having a prior experience in therapy before beginning this work is ideal. This work is appealing to people who have done prior positive work in individual therapy and still find themselves stuck. My model is primarily done within a group setting with peers.
