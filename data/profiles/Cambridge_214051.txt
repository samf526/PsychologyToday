                    <div class="statementPara">
            Dr. Clyman provides individual, couple and family therapy. His primary areas of focus are depression and anxiety, and management of stress due to trauma, relationship or work difficulties and major life phase transitions. Divorce, parental issues and the death/dying process are additional specialties. Dr. Clyman attained his doctorate in psychology (Psy.D) from Yeshiva University.         </div>
    
                    <div class="statementPara">
